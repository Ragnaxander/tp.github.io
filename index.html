Privacy policy

We take care to safeguard the personal data of our users. Any personal data accessed by the application is used to improve the application’s functionality and quality. No personal data is transferred to third parties.

What personal data do we access and for what purpose?

  • We access the name on your account and use it to synchronize your application’s data with our server and to back up the application's data. This data is transferred over an encrypted connection.
  • We access the storage on your device in order to back up the application’s data to your device.
  • We access the camera and a media library on your device in order to enable you to add your own images to the application.
  • Some versions of the application use Ads id to display advertising.
  • Some versions of the application use Google analytics id to collect information about technical errors in the application.

Last Updated: February 8, 2018 We reserve the right to change our Privacy Policy in the future without prior notice. Contact us at pis.mobile.dev@gmail.com if you have further questions.
